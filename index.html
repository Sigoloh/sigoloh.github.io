About me

Hello, I'm a software developer specializing in JavaScript and TypeScript. My coding journey started in 2012 with C programming videos on YouTube, overcoming the challenge of limited Portuguese resources.

Transitioning to JavaScript, I honed my skills by developing practical projects like CRUD applications and CMS. Venturing into more complex endeavors, I automated telephony tasks, enabling targeted campaigns and streamlined lead management for my employer.

Simultaneously, I led the development of a GPON network data collection system. This project significantly enhanced our company's ability to monitor equipment statuses at our customer home, demonstrating the impactful intersection of technology and business.

In my work, I prioritize code reliability, task automation, and seamless ecosystem integration. Leveraging tools like Docker for streamlined development environments, I ensure efficiency in every project.

Beyond technical prowess, my innate communication skills and passion for collaboration have been integral to my success. I look forward to contributing this unique blend of expertise to future software development challenges. From C programming videos to complex automation projects, my journey has molded me into a versatile and accomplished developer.
